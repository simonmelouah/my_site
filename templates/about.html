About Me



Education and Experience


In 2014 I graduated with a second class honours degree in Physics with Astronomy at Dublin City University. Proceeding this, I decided to pursue a postgraduate Higher Diploma in Computer Science from Dublin Business School, which I was awarded a first class honours for. I am now currently working as a software engineer for a company called Alkimii, building web applications in Python Flask and Ruby on Rails. I have also recently passed the AWS developer associate exam and am AWS certified (this given site is in fact configured on an AWS EC2 instance connected to a MySQL RDS database).






Interests


“I do not think there is any thrill that can go through the human heart like that felt by the inventor as he sees some creation of the brain unfolding to success . . . Such emotions make a man forget food, sleep, friends, love, everything.”

Nikola Tesla.

From a young age, I've always been interested in problem solving and the sciences which is why I decided to pursue a degree in Physics with Astronomy. Although I found the study of the universe awe inspiring (and still do!) the required focus and discipline as well as the satisfaction of resulting success in mathematical and programming modules taught in the degree really appealed to me. Due to this, upon completion of my BSc, I decided to pursue further studies into computer science which has landed me in the magical world of software development.


“Most good programmers do programming not because they expect to get paid or get adulation by the public, but because it is fun to program”

Linus Torvalds.

There's been many times in both my academic and working life that I've been thrown into the deep end with regards to both engineering and building systems in technologies I wasn't comfortable/experienced with, but as a result, increased my ability to self learn dramatically as well as gave me the determination and a "whatever it takes" attitude to get things over the line on time. I thoroughly enjoy and invite being taken out of my comfort zone with projects because, aside from the sheer enjoyment of trying to figure things out, I know it will help improve my skillset further.
